Bash Shell
===============

Important Files
------------------
* `~/.bashrc`
* `~/.bash_profile`

## FAQs

### Q. How to get the size of a file?

    cat filename | wc -c

### Q. How to do basic condition tests in bash?
Use the `test` command. See `man test`.
Use its `[[ expr ]]` format with `if`, `for` and `while` constructs.
