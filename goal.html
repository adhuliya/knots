Research Goal

Automate the inter-analyses interaction when information from an analysis B can potentially make analysis A more precise. The soundness of A is not affected by the availability of B. In other words, semantics of A is independent of B.

When the availability of information from an analysis D affects the soundness of analysis C then D is its inseparable part. Hence, it cannot be a target for automation. In other words, D is hard coded into the semantics of C.
