Research Thoughts

  1. An analysis becomes imprecise, in the following cases,
    1. Presence of non-useful statements i.e. dead code.
    2. Presence of indeterminate control jumps.
    3. Presence of expressions that it cannot compute precisely.
      • <unaryop> a
      • a <arthop> b
      • a <relop> b
      • a <otherops> b
      • *x
    4. Use of heap data structures.
